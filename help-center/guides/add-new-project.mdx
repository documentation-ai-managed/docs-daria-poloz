---
title: How to Add a New Project
description: A quick tutorial to create documentation projects.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    ````
    Go to https://docs.dariapoloz.com/dashboard and sign in.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Project">
    ````
    Click the "New Project" button on the dashboard.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name & Save">
    ````
    Enter your project name and choose a template. Click "Create" to finish.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
