## First Steps

1. Sign in at `https://docs.dariapoloz.com`
2. Click "Create Project" on your dashboard
3. Name your project and select a template

## Organize Docs

- Use the sidebar to add sections
- Drag & drop pages to reorder
- Click the "Publish" button to share
