title: Cannot Edit Documentation
description: Fix common editing issues.
---

## Symptoms

- Edit button is disabled
- Unsaved changes warning

## Causes

- Not signed in
- Lost connection
- Permission settings

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Login">
    ````
    Make sure you are signed in to your account.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reload or Reconnect">
    ````
    Refresh your browser or check your internet connection.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Permissions">
    ````
    Ask the project owner for edit access if needed.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
